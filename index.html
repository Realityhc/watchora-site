Watchora

```

A simple, local-first app for personally tracking anime, movies, and TV series in one library.

Features

  • One personal library for anime, movies, and TV series
  • Watch status and episode progress
  • Personal ratings and local notes
  • Custom lists for organizing saved media
  • Searchable titles, metadata, and poster artwork
  • Discover recommendations powered by remote media metadata
  • Local-first storage with no required account

Privacy-first by design

  • No advertisements
  • No subscriptions or in-app purchases
  • No user accounts required
  • No tracking across apps or websites
  • Your library, lists, ratings, progress, and notes stay on your device
Preparing for version 1.0

Watchora is planned as a free, non-commercial application with no advertisements, subscriptions, or in-app purchases.

Watchora uses TMDb metadata and poster images through the Watchora backend. This product uses the TMDb API but is not endorsed or certified by TMDb.
```
